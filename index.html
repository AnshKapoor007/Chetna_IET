Events

Day 1

  • Melodie Artistica
    The Rangoli making competition, in which students will showcase their creativity and attachment with their culture.
  • Aces of Development
    This would be a contest demanding the participants to design a webpage on their own, the best selected designers would be awarded.
  • Chess
    This is going to be a time-based, knockout-chess tournament in which individuals can participate.
  • Velorant
    Online Gaming competition.
  • Technodium
    This would be a technical quizzing contest.
  • ColoursHut
    The Painting competition, for the students to relieve stress and give encouragement to creativity.
  • BGMI/Free Fire
    Online Gaming competition.

Day 2

  • Code-A-Thon
    This would be a Data Structure and Algorithms coding contest.
  • Walk-In-Style
    This is a ramp walk event, In which the participants will show their fashion sense and style.
  • Tug of War
    Two teams against each other in a test of strength: teams pull on opposite ends of a rope, with the goal being to bring the rope a certain distance in one direction against the force of the opposing team's pull.
  • Debug Your Soul
    This is a code debugging round in which the participants need to resolve the errors that are existing in the code. The faster, the better. Accuracy would also be a pinpoint.
  • Scavenger-Hunt
    Treasure hunt event within the college building & auditorium. This will be a team event and the team(4-5 members) which finds the assigned places according to hints provided.
  • Express your Hunar
    This is a series of events consisting of:
    1. Mimicry
    2. Stand-Up Comedy
    3. Monologues and open-mic
    4. Instrumentals
    5. Storytelling
    6. Dance
    7. Singing, etc.
  • Award Ceremony
    Award distribution to the winners by the Faculty. Distribution of trophy to the alumni and certificate distribution.
  • Music Night
    A Night with Musical Instruments where everyone can dance and feel the jest of fest celebration.
